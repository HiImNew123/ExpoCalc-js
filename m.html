About Us - ExpoCalc

ExpoCalc is an educational technology with a mission to ensure a fun learning experience for students and build a greater relationship with the subject.

Our vision for the future of the Mathematics field is to be able to catch up to the fast-paced world without disregarding learning ethics.

We are building a safe and beneficial website, but will also consider our mission fulfilled if our work aids others to achieve this outcome.
