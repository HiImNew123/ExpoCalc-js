Definition of Terms

Definition: In a mathematical expression, a coefficient is the numerical part of a term. Access to My Data For instance, in the quadratic function ax² + bx + c, 'a,' 'b,' and 'c' stand for coefficients.
2.Parabola:
Definition: The parabola is a \"U-shaped\" curve in a graph of a quadratic function. The sign of the coefficient of the x² term determines the direction in which it opens( upwards or downwards).
3.Vertex:
Definition: A vertex of a parabola is the maximum or minimum point on a curve. The position of the vertex is critically important for the behavior of the quadratic function to be known.
4.Range:
Definition: In mathematics, the range of a function is the set of all possible output values. For a quadratic function the range is determined by the y-coordinate of the vertex.
